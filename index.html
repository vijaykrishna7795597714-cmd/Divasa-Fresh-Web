Brand Overview

Divasa Fresh is a farm-to-market fresh produce supply platform operating in India. We work with partner farmers to source vegetables and fruits through structured procurement, quality assurance, and disciplined logistics.

Purpose & Vision

Our Purpose: To build a reliable and efficient fresh produce supply ecosystem that benefits both farmers and end customers.

Our Vision: To become a trusted farm-to-market supply partner across India by setting standards in sourcing, quality, and operational excellence.

What We Do

We source produce from partner farms, aggregate and grade it using defined protocols, and fulfill household, retail, and HORECA requirements through predictable delivery schedules and structured operations.
